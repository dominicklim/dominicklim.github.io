Nimbus Messenger

Teammates: Ronak Vora, Kevin Yao

Towards the end of my senior year, I co-founded Nimbus Messenger, an iOS content-sharing app with the structure of email threads and the dynamics of instant messaging.

It was a fun, challenging, and educational experience. We were fortunate to have some enthusiastic users and interested investors, but ultimately decided to wind the project down.

Slack added a similar feature a year and a half later.
