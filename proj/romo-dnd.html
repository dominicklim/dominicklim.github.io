Drag and Drop Programming

I hacked together this drag and drop programming environment at Romotive when we were trying to figure out ways to integrate programming into our product. Users would create, upload, and save programs to send off to their Romo. This programming environment also provided an opportunity for users without Romos to still enjoy him.

It was featured in this TED talk that Keller gave about Romo.

Ultimately, we decided to go with on-device programming which was a major component of the Romo iOS app.

You can check it out here.
