Upshot!

I built Upshot! while visiting family in the Philippines. My brother is an avid archer, and he brought along his equipment to practice and teach us how to shoot. While my brother practiced, I kept score, and was amazed that there were no simple apps that streamline the tedious process. That's why I built Upshot!

You can check it out here.

Built with: Objective-C
